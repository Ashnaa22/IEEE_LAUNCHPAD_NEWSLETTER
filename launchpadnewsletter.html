Updates

Complete Event Roadmap

STEP1

Online Registration: Candidates start by registering on the Launchpad Kerala website. Upon successful registration, they secure their place in the event.

STEP 2

Confirmation: Within 48 hours of registration, candidates will receive a confirmation email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team. This email will contain information about the next steps, which will also be available on the updates page of the website

STEP 3

Join the Community: Candidates are encouraged to join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server, where they can participate in the Launchpad Challenges. Details for joining can be found on the provided GitHub link.

STEP 4

Technical Challenge: Once on the Mulearn Discord Server, candidates will find various levels and tasks under the IEEE Launchpad category in the #ieee-launchpad-announcement channel. The challenge is divided into three levels:
Level 1: Foundational Skills and Introduction
Level 2: Specialized Learning Paths and Challenges
Level 3:Industry-curated Challenges and Hackathons
complete and submit their tasks on time to earn Karma points. Daily office hours for support will be held from 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm in the #ieee-launchpad-officehours channel. Progress can be tracked using the digital identity in the #ieee-launchpad-leaderboard.

STEP 5

Online Aptitude Test: Candidates will then take an online assessment to showcase their competencies to potential employers.

STEP 6

Await the Finalized Results: Results will be announced after a thorough evaluation of candidates' performance in both the Technical Challenge and the online test. This evaluation will open up exciting opportunities based on their achievements.

STEP 7

Final Interview:The final stage is an interview, providing candidates with the chance to showcase their skills and make a lasting impression on recruiters. This step is crucial for securing their desired job.
